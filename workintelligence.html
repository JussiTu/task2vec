How it works

  1. Paste a ticket or task into a chat-like interface.
  2. Upload strategy documents (roadmap, principles, OKRs).
  3. The assistant asks clarifying questions only when needed.
  4. We save a structured task vector: tech, product, method, invention, risk, intent.
  5. You get alignment, proposals, similar work, and best contacts—with evidence.
